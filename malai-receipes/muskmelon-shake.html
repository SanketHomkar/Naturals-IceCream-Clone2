Muskmelon Shake

Ingredients :

  • 1 cup muskmelon (chopped)
  • 1 tsp gond katira (soaked)
  • 1 tsp sabza/basil seeds (soaked)
  • 4–5 cashews (soaked)
  • A few strands of saffron (soaked)
  • 2 scoops Naturals Malai Ice Cream

Method :

  1. In a blender, add chopped muskmelon, soaked cashews, saffron, and 2 scoops of Naturals Malai Ice Cream.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass, add soaked gond katira and basil seeds on top.
  4. Serve chilled!
