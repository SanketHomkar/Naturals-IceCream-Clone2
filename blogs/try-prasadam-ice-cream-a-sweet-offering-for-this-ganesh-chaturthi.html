That moment when the delicious aroma of coconut, banana, and cardamom drifts through the air, and you know something delicious is on its way? This Ganesh Chaturthi, Naturals Ice Cream has something that will make that moment even better: the return of its exclusive, limited-edition Prasadam Ice Cream. It’s the festival’s traditional prasad reimagined as a decadent, hand-churned ice cream. One bite and you’ll feel like you’ve stepped right into the heart of the celebrations, even if you’re miles away from home.


Turning tradition into a treat


Ganesh Chaturthi is all about devotion, family, and of course, sweets; especially the famous modak. Soft, steamed, and filled with coconut and jaggery, it’s known as Lord Ganesha’s favourite. Naturals has taken the essence of this offering and transformed it into something new while keeping all its festive charm.


Prasadam Ice Cream blends tender coconut, ripe bananas, plump kismis, and a gentle sprinkle of cardamom, all in Naturals’ signature milk and sugar base. Every ingredient has a story, rooted in the traditions of the festival. The coconut and banana are both common offerings during poojas, the raisins add a joyful chew in every bite, and the cardamom ties everything together with its warm, unmistakable taste in a chilled, scoopable form.


An experience in every spoon


The first bite greets you with the tenderness of tender coconut, instantly reminding you of the sweet fillings in festive modaks. Then comes the mellow sweetness of banana, adding a smooth body to the ice cream. Just as you’re enjoying that, a raisin pops in for a little burst of chewiness, and the cardamom lingers in the background like a soft festive melody.


It’s like enjoying prasadam straight from the temple, only cooler (literally).


Perfect for every festive moment


Prasadam Ice Cream fits beautifully into any Ganesh Chaturthi celebration. Hosting family after evening prayers? Serve it in little bowls as a sweet surprise. Heading out to see the pandals? Take it in a cone and make it part of your festive stroll. Craving something filling and chilled? Order it as a thick milkshake from your nearest Naturals outlet.


Some people even place a cup of Prasadam Ice Cream on their offering tray before sharing it with guests: a sweet and modern twist on the idea of prasadam. It’s a simple gesture, but one that adds a unique touch to the celebrations.


Here only for Ganesh Chaturthi


Part of the magic of Prasadam Ice Cream is that it’s here for a short time. Just like Ganesh Chaturthi, it arrives with great excitement and leaves you looking forward to its return. Once the festival ends, the flavour disappears from the menu until next year. That exclusivity makes it all the more special, a little reminder to enjoy the moment while it lasts.


Make it part of your celebration


Ganesh Chaturthi is a time for joy, togetherness, and the sharing of blessings. Prasadam Ice Cream puts all of that in a single scoop. It’s festive, it’s full of flavour, and it carries the warmth of tradition in every bite.
