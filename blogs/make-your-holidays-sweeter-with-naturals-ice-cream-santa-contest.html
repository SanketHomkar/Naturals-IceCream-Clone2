Make Your Holidays Sweeter with Natural’s Ice Cream Santa Contest

Blog thumbnail Make Your Holidays Sweeter with Natural’s Ice Cream Santa Contest Blog thumbnailJanuary 5, 20262025-12-23T09:11:54+05:30Blog The holiday season is a wonderful time of year, filled with celebration and simple pleasures. This year, Natural’s Ice Cream is adding an exciting element to your festive moments by bringing back their annual Ice Cream Santa contest. It is a chance to win significant prizes simply by enjoying your favourite ice cream.


Running for 12 days across all stores , the Ice Cream Santa contest begins on 21st December 2025 and continues till 1st January 2026, making it the perfect way to celebrate the year-end festivities and ring in the New Year with something special.


For years, Ice Cream Santa has delighted customers, turning a simple ice cream purchase into a high-stakes, yet fun, competition. It’s a massive lucky draw where any 500 grams family pack size gives you a chance to celebrate the New Year with a major prize. This tradition is Natural’s way of spreading maximum cheer and rewarding the loyalty of their customers during the giving season.

Here is how you can enter the lucky draw and potentially win big:

The Sweet and Simple Way to Win


Participating in the Ice Cream Santa contest is straightforward, ensuring that every customer has a fair chance at the giveaways. Here is how you can enter the lucky draw:

  • Visit Your Favourite Store : Head to your nearest Natural’s Ice Cream outlet and purchase two 500 grams family packs of your preferred flavours.
  • Locate the QR Code : After your purchase, check your bill. It will contain a special QR Code which serves as your contest entry mechanism
  • Scan and Submit : Use your smartphone to scan the QR code. You will be directed to a short, secure entry page. Fill in the necessary details, submit the form, and stand a chance to win exciting rewards such as a Smart TV, refrigerator, gift vouchers, and more.

It truly is that simple. The contest operates as a genuine lucky draw, meaning every valid entry has an equal chance to win. There are no complicated challenges, just the opportunity for simple luck while enjoying your ice cream.


Consider the possibilities. Today you might be tasting the fresh flavour of tender coconut ice cream, and tomorrow you could be planning your dream holiday or anticipating the delivery of a new home appliance. These attractive rewards are available through the contest.


So, this holiday season, enjoy your favourite natural ice cream and take a shot at winning something big. Visit Natural’s today, purchase two 500 grams family packs of any flavour, scan the code, and see if Ice Cream Santa brings you a wonderful surprise. Your next incredible prize is literally just a scan away!
