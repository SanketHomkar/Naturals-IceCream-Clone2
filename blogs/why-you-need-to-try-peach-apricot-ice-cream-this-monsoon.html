Why You Need to Try Peach Apricot Ice Cream This Monsoon?

Monsoon is the perfect season for slow indulgences, warm conversations, and surprise comfort finds, and this Naturals’ Peach Apricot flavour checks all those boxes. With a combination of two complementary fruits, a creamy texture, and Naturals’ signature commitment to quality, Peach Apricot is a seasonal gem that’s absolutely worth your attention.


A flavour that feels like a soft hug


The pairing of peach and apricot may not be the most obvious choice on the menu, but it’s one of the most harmonious. Peach brings a subtle, mellow sweetness with floral notes, while apricot adds a gentle tang that gives the flavour its edge. Together, they create a sweet-and-sour profile that is light, refreshing, and incredibly moreish.


The richness of the milk base rounds out the fruitiness without overshadowing it. With each spoonful, you get a balance of fruit-forward flavour that makes it ideal for the rainy season, when your cravings are a little more laid-back and comforting.


Made from real fruit, the Naturals way


As with every flavour at Naturals, Peach Apricot Ice Cream is made using the brand’s core philosophy: seasonal ingredients with no artificial colours, flavours, or preservatives.


Each batch contains plump peaches and apricots blended with milk and sugar. That’s it. No syrups, no shortcuts, just the honest taste of fruits done right.


The peaches and apricots used are chosen at peak ripeness, ensuring maximum flavour and inherent sweetness. The fruits are then churned, giving the ice cream a lovely-textured bite. That gentle fruit texture is part of what makes the flavour feel so close to the real thing.


Naturals has always maintained that fewer ingredients mean better taste. Peach Apricot Ice Cream is proof of that belief in action. It tastes authentic, and absolutely unprocessed, just like a homemade ice cream made from fruits bought at your local market.


A seasonal scoop worth waiting for


Peach Apricot is a seasonal flavour. It typically arrives during the monsoon months, when both peaches and apricots are available in their prime. As Naturals doesn’t use off-season produce, this ice cream can only be made in small batches when the fruit is just right. That makes it all the more special.


The timing couldn’t be better. The rainy season often dulls our appetite for heavy desserts or piping-hot snacks. Instead, we look for cooler, milder options that still feel indulgent. Peach Apricot Ice Cream fits beautifully into that monsoon mood. It’s chilled but not icy, fruity but not overpowering, and luxurious without being rich.
